Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Dumisani Tukula, a passionate Graphic Designer, Front-End Developer, and Digital Entrepreneur based in Blantyre, Malawi. With expertise in creating visually stunning designs and functional websites, I specialize in full-stack solutions using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB.

My journey began at 17 with a love for visual storytelling and music creation. I've developed projects like MsikaOnline, an online marketplace, and Dynamité Digital, my professional brand showcasing skills in graphic design and web development. I also enjoy transforming ideas into reality, such as building community organization sites and unique dating platforms with futuristic themes.

When not coding or designing, I'm making music, exploring photography, or collaborating on innovative projects. I believe in continuous learning and leveraging tools like Firebase and Postman to deliver high-quality, SEO-optimized digital experiences.
